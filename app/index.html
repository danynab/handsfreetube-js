HandsfreeTube

How to use it.

You can change videos with your hands.

Try it! Move your hand to both sides.

Also, you can change the volume with your hands.

Try it! Move your hand up and down.

And finally, the voice is very important too.

Next are described the avaliable voice commands.

Try them! Say the commands.

look for Look for a playlist. playlist Show the playlist.
play Start the video. stop Stop the video.
number X Select the option X. help Show help menu.
