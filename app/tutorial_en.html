HandsfreeTube

How to use it.

You can change videos with your hands and

you can hide the search sidebar and the playlist sidebar.

Try the recognition! Move your hand to both sides.

Also, you can change the volume with your hands and

you can scroll the results and the playlist.

Try the recognition! Move your hand up and down.

The voice is very important too.

Next are described the avaliable voice commands.

Try them! Say the commands.

look for Look for a playlist. playlist Show the playlist.
play Start the video. stop Stop the video.
number X Select the option X. help Show help menu.

But, to do voice commands you must notice me.

Move your hand from one side to other side. Try it!
